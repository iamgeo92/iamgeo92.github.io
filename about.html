The oral health analytics platform for anyone, anywhere



DentalSmarts is a mobile app in the teledentistry space which helps individuals take preventative care of their teeth in a cost-effective manner. Our app leverages artificial intelligence and remote dentistry to inform users of potential issues in their teeth and recommend treatment options.



Our mission: Improve oral health through personalized analytics

In the past decade, the number of ER visits due to dental related problems has doubled. 80% of these issues could have been avoided with regular dental visits. Additionally, new research is indicating associations between oral infections and heart and lung diseases, stroke, premature births and diabetes. The problem is that 51% of Americans didn’t visit the dentist in the past year and didn’t learn about any lingering issues. They avoided the dentist due to a low perceived need, high costs and a lack of time. We believe that a convenient and cost-effective app that lets users analyze the state of their oral health can create a sense of urgency in the user's mind, get them into the dentist's office and prevent acute problems.
